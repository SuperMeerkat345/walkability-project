WALKABILITY FACTORS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Density - Crucial property of walkability, concentrates more people and places within walking distance.

Pedestrian infrastructure:
- Sidewalks and walkways (Safe, well maintained, well lit)
- Crosswalks and pedestrian crossings (Clearly marked)
- Pedestrian signals (Safe to cross a street)
- Benches 

Safety and Security:
- Minimal traffic (Secure from traffic dangers)
- Traffic calming measures (Speed bumps, traffic circles, raised sidewalks)
- Lightposts providing well lit streets (Enhances visibility improving safety at night)

Aesthetics and comfort: 
- Pleasant environment (Landscaping, public art)
- Greenery (Trees provide shade and weather protection)
- Clean streets (No trash)
